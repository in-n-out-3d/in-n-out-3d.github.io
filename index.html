In-N-Out: Face Video Inversion and Editing with Volumetric Decomposition

Abstract

GAN inversion technologies enable creative image and video editing effects. Recently, 3D-aware GANs that have explicit camera control with 3D consistency open up new opportunities for creative contents editing. However, due to the training limitations of GANs, existing GAN inversion approaches still have difficulty in the out-of-distribution (OOD) data. In this paper, we propose a novel 3D-aware face video editing system with the presence of OOD objects. Our main idea is to split a video into two neural radiance fields, in-distribution, and out-of-distribution, and compose them together for the reconstruction. During editing, our approach allows us only to edit the in-distribution part to preserve the editability better. We evaluate our method's reconstruction accuracy and editability on real videos and showcase favorable results against other baselines.
